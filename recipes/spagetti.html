Spagetti!

This recipe has been handed down from my mother. It is a family favorite and will not be replaced! (Definite husband pleaser!)

For this spaghetti, we turn to our favorite quick meat sauce recipe. Itâ€™s made using one-pot, is ultra flavorful, and can be made in under 45 minutes.

The spaghetti sauce is also perfect for making ahead. It can be refrigerated for up to three days and stored in your freezer for about three months.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
  3. Drain grease
  4. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
